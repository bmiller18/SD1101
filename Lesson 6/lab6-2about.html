Playstation History

プレイステーション

Developed and Owned by Sony Interactive Entertainment. The PlayStation originates in Tokyo, Japan and was introduced December 3, 1994. PlayStation was the first console of any type to ship over 100 millions units under a decade. The PlayStation 2 is known for being the best selling console of all time as of 2015. PlayStation continues to be one of the top selling consoles today. PlayStation 5 has a planned release date of Holiday 2020.
